Preprints

  • Reflection Theory of Nichols Algebras over Coquasi-Hopf Algebras with Bijective Antipode
    B. Li, G. Liu. arXiv:2602.07946.

    Abstract:We investigate the reflection theory of Nichols algebras over arbitrary coquasi-Hopf algebras with bijective antipode, generalizing previous results restricted to the pointed cosemisimple setting \cite{reflection1}. By establishing a braided monoidal equivalence between categories of rational Yetter-Drinfeld modules via a dual pair, we demonstrate that a tuple of finite-dimensional irreducible Yetter-Drinfeld modules admitting all reflections gives rise to a semi-Cartan graph. As an application, we consider an explicit example of a rank three Nichols algebra from \cite{huang2024classification}. We show that it yields a standard Cartan graph and prove that it is, in fact, an affine Nichols algebra.
